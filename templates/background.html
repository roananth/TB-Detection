About Tuberculosis

Tuberculosis (TB) is an infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis. It primarily affects the lungs but can also impact other parts of the body. TB is a serious global health issue that can be fatal if left untreated. Early detection and treatment are critical.

Our model helps identify signs of tuberculosis in chest scans, assisting healthcare providers in early diagnosis and treatment planning.
